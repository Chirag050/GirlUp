Events

Reflecting on "Better Than Barbie": A Celebration of Women and Empowerment The Better Than Barbie event presented by GirlUp was a hit, leaving everyone reflecting on the experience and the positive impact it had. The goal was to honour the diverse qualities that make women exceptional and to promote a message of equality and empowerment. Attendees enjoyed a variety of food stalls offering delicious treats, explored unique jewellery pieces, and were thrilled by a lively flashmob performance that added an exciting burst of energy. Better Than Barbie was a testament to GirlUp’s commitment to empowering women and fostering equality.Together, let’s continue to champion a future where every human’s brilliance is celebrated and valued.

Our Projects

Empowering Young Minds: Girl Up Society's Visit to Senior Secondary Smart School, Tripuri Girl Up Society recently visited Senior Secondary Smart School, Tripuri, to promote personal safety and awareness. Our interactive session on recognizing good and bad touch, supported by storytelling and role-playing, encouraged open discussion and trust. We also organized engaging activities, providing students a platform to express themselves. We’re grateful to the school staff and students for their enthusiastic participation, and we remain committed to building a safer community..

About Us

Girl Up is a youth-led organization dedicated to empowering girls and young women through education, advocacy, and leadership development. We aim to create a world where every girl is safe, educated, and equipped to reach her full potential. By organizing community events, workshops, and outreach programs, we focus on building confidence, raising awareness on critical issues, and fostering a supportive environment for girls to thrive. Together, we’re shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
